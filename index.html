Hello, and welcome to my podcast! My name is Elise Brooklynn, and this microblog was inspired by a quote of St. Thérèse of Lisieux:

“The only way I can prove my love is by scattering flowers and these flowers are every little sacrifice, every glance and word, and the doing of the least actions for love.”

Through the daily bible readings of the Mass, I hope to scatter flowers every day as a little sacrifice of love.
